Hi! You have reached the webpage of Dr. Richard Smith. I am a postdoctoral researcher at NOIRLab's Gemini North Observatory in Hilo, Hawaii, USA. My current research focuses on variable stars in the Andromeda Galaxy using Hubble Space Telescope data. I graduated from my doctoral program at Queen's University Belfast in July 2023. My thesis investigated the properties of trans-Neptunian Binaries using archival Hubble Space Telescope observations.
